My Path

I started filming at 14, documenting my parkour sessions with friends, and quickly realized I loved being behind the camera more than anything else

That early curiosity shaped my approach today: I believe deeply in the connection between directing and editing, and I still enjoy being hands-on with every project. Sound, emotion, and a strong visual identity are at the core of my work

My creative journey — from the docufilm Bruciasse il cielo to the Desnuda project shot in Colombia — has been especially meaningful, built on trust and shared intuition. Filmmaking for me is about authenticity, collaboration, and creating something that feels alive
